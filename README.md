# Gamers Club Masters 2026

Gamers Club Masters 2026 is a Counter-Strike 2 tournament organised and operated by Gamers Club as part of the company’s 10-year celebration.

The tournament marks the return of one of Gamers Club’s main competitive properties and will be played as a fully offline event at the Gamers Club studio in Sorocaba, São Paulo, Brazil.

---

## Tournament Overview

- **Tournament Name:** Gamers Club Masters 2026  
- **Game:** Counter-Strike 2  
- **Tournament Tier:** Ranked — Tier 2  
- **Tournament Organiser:** Gamers Club 
- **Region:** South America  
- **Location:**  
  - Online Open Qualifiers  
  - Offline Main Event at Gamers Club Studio, Sorocaba, São Paulo, Brazil

## Valve Ranking System (VRS) — Additional Information

This section constitutes the **Additional Information** required under Valve Tournament Operation Requirements.

- **VRS Subdivision Used:**  
  - South America

- **VRS Starting Rank:**  
  - Highest-ranked eligible Roster within the applicable VRS subdivision

- **Number of Direct VRS Invites:**  
  - 4 Rosters

- **Invite Date:**  
  - To be confirmed

- **VRS Publication Used:**  
  - To be confirmed

- **Visa Region:**  
  - Brazil

- **Integrity Disqualification Rules:**  
  - Rosters with active VAC bans  
  - Rosters violating Gamers Club Anti-Cheat policies  
  - Rosters failing eligibility requirements as defined in the official rulebook   

Invitations are issued sequentially according to the VRS order.  
Invited Rosters must accept or decline the invitation within the response period defined by the organiser.  
If a Roster declines, fails to respond or cannot attend the offline Main Event, the invitation is passed to the next eligible Roster until all four (4) direct invitation slots are filled.

The final list of invited Rosters will be published publicly and will not be altered except as required to maintain compliance with Valve Tournament Operation Requirements.

## Playoffs

- **Teams:** 4  
- **Bracket:** Single Elimination  

**Semifinals**
